# Aero Convert™: Zero Click Currency & Unit Conversion

Aero Convert is a lightweight, privacy-first Chrome Extension that lets you instantly convert currencies and units simply by highlighting text on any webpage. No clicks, no popups, no context-switching.

## ✨ Features

- **Zero-Click Conversions:** Highlight a price or measurement (e.g., "$100" or "50 miles") and see the conversion instantly in a clean, floating tooltip.
- **Smart Detection:** Automatically handles complex formatting, shared currency symbols, and colloquial names (e.g., "Swiss Francs" to CHF).
- **Live & Historical Rates:** Supports over 160 world currencies with live exchange rates and 7-day trend sparklines.
- **Unit Conversions:** Instantly convert length, weight, temperature, volume, area, speed, and more.
- **Privacy First:** 100% local processing. No tracking, no analytics, no accounts required.

## 🔒 Privacy

Aero Convert respects your data. It does not collect or transmit any of your browsing history or highlighted text. All calculations are done locally on your device.
